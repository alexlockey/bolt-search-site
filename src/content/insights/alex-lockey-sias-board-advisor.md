Bolt Search is delighted to share some exciting news - Alex Lockey, Managing Partner at Bolt, has joined the [SIAS](https://siasuk.com/) Board as an Advisor.

SIAS is a trailblazer in technical skills and apprenticeship assessments, specialising in STEM industries, future tech, and the net-zero transition. With their innovative, customer-focused approach, they've quickly earned a solid reputation for driving growth and adding genuine value to the apprenticeship sector.

At a time when technical skills and specialised training are more important than ever, SIAS is dedicated to boosting workforce qualifications and supporting industry evolution. With a clear mission to transform technical education and future-proof the labour market, they're not afraid to push the boundaries.

Alex's appointment, along with [Anthony Ogden, Managing Director Operations
- Croda Pharma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tonyogdenuk/), is a strategic move that underscores SIAS's commitment to dynamic growth and talent development. It's a big step towards cementing SIAS as a leader in skills and workforce development.

## SIAS - A Trusted Name in Technical Skills

SIAS has rapidly become a trusted name in technical skills accreditation, thanks to its strong commitment to understanding and meeting the needs of training providers, apprentices, and industry partners.

They have a fresh, disruptive approach that challenges traditional methods of awarding and assessment. Their bold vision for growth, recognises the huge opportunity and important role they can play in shaping qualification frameworks in critical sectors for the future economy, globally.

With the demand for STEM talent and skills in net-zero industries on the rise, SIAS is ideally placed to make a lasting impact on education, employment, and economic mobility.
